Welcome

Texas Linguistics Society 10 (TLSX) is a graduate-student run conference in linguistics to be held over November 3-5 2006. The theme of TLSX is the application of techniques from computational linguistics to descriptive linguistics and the analysis of less-studied languages.

See you Friday!

The conference is kicking off Friday morning at the University Teaching Center (UTC) 3.124. Friday afternoon we move to UTC 3.104.

Saturday and Sunday the conference is being held at Calhoun (CAL) 100. This is on the ground floor of Calhoun, entrance from the outside.
