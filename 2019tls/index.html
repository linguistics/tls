Travel & Hospitality

Venue

TLS will be held at the University of Texas at Austin in the Julius Glickman Conference Center. [google maps]

The UT is located just a few miles north of downtown Austin. It is accessible primarily via Guadalupe St. which runs along the entire west side of the main campus.

Travel

The main airport servicing Austin, TX and the surrounding area is the Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS). Shuttles and taxis are available from AUS, and the route 20 bus has multiple stops near campus.
