Texas Linguistics Society 9

Friday, November 4 - Sunday, November 6, 2005

University of Texas - Austin

For nearly a decade, the Texas Linguistics Society's annual conference has provided a forum for language scholars to learn from one another and from respected researchers in their fields. This year’s conference theme focuses on the morphosyntax of underrepresented languages, a topic which is of interest to people working in many fields of language study, including syntax, semantics, morphology, typology, descriptive & documentary linguistics, and historical linguistics.
