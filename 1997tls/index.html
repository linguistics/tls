Texas Linguistics Society


The Texas Linguistics Society is a recently formed organization made up of graduate and undergraduate students interested in the study of language from a theoretical perspective. The proceedings for last years conference, The Syntax and Semantics of Predication, will be available in the fall of 1997 through Texas Linguistic Forum. Plans are currently being made for the 1998 conference of the Texas Linguistics Society, which will be held MARCH 13-15, 1998 at The University of Texas at Austin. The topic of the conference will be:

Exploring the Boundaries Between Phonetics and Phonology
