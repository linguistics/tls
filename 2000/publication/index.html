TLS 2000 Publication

Selected papers presented at TLS 2000 are currently being solicited for publication in the resultant volume, which we are very happy to say will be published by Cambridge University Press.

Reference

The working reference for the volume is:
Meier, Richard P., Cormier, Kearsy A., & Quinto, David G. In prep. Modality and Structure in Signed and Spoken Languages. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press.
